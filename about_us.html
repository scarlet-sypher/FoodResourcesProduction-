About Us

We are the students of Lovely Professnional University

We are currently persuing B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering and this is a collaborative work done by the students of section K23GX under the supervision of Dr. Navdeep Singh We're dedicated to fostering awareness and driving positive change in our community. Our primary objective is to spread awarness about our Food resources and their production as well. we are doing this through informative content.

The mian motive of our team is to spread awarness through this website and to provide information and resources on food, nutrition, and sustainable agriculture. This can include information on:

  1. Different types of food and their nutritional value
  2. How to choose and prepare healthy meals
  3. Sustainable food production and consumption
  4. Food security and hunger relief
  5. Recipes, cooking tips, and kitchen hacks
  6. Food safety and hygiene
  7. Food allergies and intolerances
  8. Food culture and tradition
